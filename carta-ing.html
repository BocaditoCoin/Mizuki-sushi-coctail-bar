Anisakis Prevention

Prevención Anisakis

Fish or cephalopod molluscs served raw or undercooked in this establishment comply with the provisions of Real Decreto 1021/2022, of 13th of December, about the prevention of anisakis.

Butterfish Warning: Butterfish may cause gastrointestinal effects. It is recommended to consult with the restaurant staff in case of doubt about any ingredient or allergen.
